Android 101

Android 101 is for you who want to learn about the Android platform, but have not yet gotten started. We will provide an introduction to several Android components, in addition to network communication, so you will be able to develop applications on your own.

Kurset er delt i en teoretisk del og en praktisk del. I den praktiske delen vil du lage en twitter-aktig applikasjon som integrerer mot en RESTful backend.
